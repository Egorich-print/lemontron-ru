Pitch

I’ve been passionately creating a 3D printer that powerful as it is portable. Lemontron fits neatly into a standard filament box, breaking boundaries and making it the perfect tool for makers on the go.

Whether you’re a seasoned 3D printing enthusiast or just getting started, Lemontron offers something brand new. It’s easy to assemble, disassemble, and store, without sacrificing the performance you expect from a full-sized 3D printer. With Lemontron, you can take your zest for printing anywhere.
